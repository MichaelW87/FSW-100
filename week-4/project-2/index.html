About Coma

SqueezedComa Sweet's name came to me when I was baking one day. I have been an amature baker for many years mostly for family and friends. One day I was making a cake and thought I would turn it in to a side business and would bring in an element from my gaming world, something that is tied to me and me alone. I came up with my gamer tag and bring about SqueezedComa Sweet's. I have a passion for baking and gaming and I want to bring them together and one day do game inspired sweet. I would also like to share my passion with everyone. enjoy the browsing and feel free to reach out.
